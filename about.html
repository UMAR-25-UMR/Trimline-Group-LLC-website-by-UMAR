Our Story

Trimline Group, established in 1977, is a pioneer in global supply chain solutions and diversified business operations. With nearly five decades of excellence behind it, the Group has grown into a diversified business powerhouse with a growing focus on e-commerce and digital consumer brands.

Our international trade business unit is divided into various companies including Polymers, Chemicals Jade chemi and AgriCommodities.
