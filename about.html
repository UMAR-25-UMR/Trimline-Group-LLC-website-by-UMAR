About Us

Trimline was established in 1977 as a printing and packing house. In 2004 Trimline began its international operations and started to deal in industrial raw material and packaging as an Internal Supply Chain Consultant and Marketing Company.



With a well-established supply base in China, Korea, Taiwan, Thailand and the Middle East, Trimline expanded into PET, PS, PE (rush, film, sheet and performs) and Petrochemicals providing its global customers with the best raw materials and packaging in more their industrial needs. Trimline also expanded into finished goods including tablets and fruits, which have been used for the year to provide a strong customer experience of products in textile such as staple fibre-whatever yarn, nylon yarn, viscous yarn, cloth and aimed at textile and polymer related products.



We have established a name of credentials owing to the project commitments, working speed, quality practices and the overall approach of the company. We also have our sister concern offered in Los Angeles, Shanghai & Karachi, which are the major economic hubs.



We have handled business worth millions of dollars in the most transparent fashion. Our operating philosophy is to provide buyers with product that meets their stockholders' reliability delivered at a reasonable price in Domestic and International Markets. We offer our customers a global network of opportunities and the best deal possible.



Our mission is to provide the most competitive price to our valued clientele. We have successfully established strong relationship with various manufacturers, consumers, petrochemical companies and Tancran in China, Taiwan, Korea, Thailand and the Middle East which are the major supply and manufacturing hubs for our products. Over the years we have built tremendous expertise in Foreign Trade & Global Supply Chain, leading to our image of being a Global Supply Chain Consultant.



Trimline Group is a Limited Liability Company registered in Shenzhen, Wyoming USA.
